Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is eligible for this program?

    Any Degree/Btech/BE/MTech final year, Passed outs, Individuals, Employees are eligible for this program.

  • What is the duration of the program?

    The duration of the program varies depending on the course. Typically, it ranges from 3 to 6 months.

  • Do I get the assured placement?

    While we provide placement assistance and support, securing a job depends on your skills, performance, and interview results.

  • What is the basic academic percentage required to enroll for the course?

    A minimum of 50% academic percentage is recommended, but we encourage all motivated learners to apply.

  • What is the execution plan of the program?

    The program consists of online and offline sessions, hands-on projects, mentorship, and assessments to ensure practical learning.

  • Can we take this course online?

    Yes, the course is available online, allowing you to learn from anywhere at your own pace.

  • I am already employed, will I be eligible for the program?

    Yes, working professionals can enroll and learn at their convenience.

  • What if I miss the session due to an emergency?

    You will have access to recorded sessions and additional learning materials to catch up on missed classes.

  • Do you provide any certificate after the program?

    Yes, upon successful completion of the program, you will receive a certification.

  • Have suggestions for us?

    We welcome your feedback! Feel free to share your suggestions with our support team.
